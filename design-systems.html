#DesignSystems

Task: Implementation

Role: Design Lead

Tools: Figma

Creating a Global Design System

The problem our product team faced was an inconsistency across regional apps for a global car rental brand. Part of the solution was to implement a tailored design system to unify the brand across regions: EMEA, NAM, and APAC. This system would ensure a cohesive user experience for millions of users, enhancing brand recognition and operational efficiency.

Bringing Figma In-House

One challenge we faced was transitioning from Figma designs provided by an external vendor to our in-house app team. This bespoke design system improved regional team agility and deepened product understanding, but they still needed to be developed in code.

Switching from Flutter to React Native

Initially, our design system project began in collaboration with an external development agency specializing in React Native. The strategic transition aimed to unify our global development teams around a single, efficient structure for app development, ensuring consistency across regions.

Navigating the Challenges

NAM and EMEA faced divergent UI designs due to regional differences and legacy systems. The transition from Flutter to React and alignment on user flows within Figma helped overcome these challenges, ensuring brand consistency while adapting to local needs.

Anticipating the Impact

The new app is expected to streamline development processes and reduce maintenance costs through a unified codebase and design system. We are optimistic that user experience improvements will uplift Net Promoter Scores (NPS), reflecting higher user satisfaction and loyalty.

What Could Be Next?

The design system will evolve alongside our business needs. Potential innovations include AI-driven personalization, enhanced accessibility, and the integration of emerging technologies like blockchain for improved data security and compliance.
